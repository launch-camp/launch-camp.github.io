Our Mission

"If a child can't learn the way we teach, maybe we should teach the way they learn" - Ignacio Estrada

There is a fundamental disconnect between the skills students need to be successful in the 21st century and the education they're receiving. Students are both not being taught the vital skills to be competitive in the economy and not being empowered to have ownership over their learning and their education. Rather, they're often pushed through a system that prioritizes and rewards measurable skills.



Launch Studio's mission is to give Title I schools the resources and support to provide students with a fundamentally different educational experience, where they are empowered to take ownership over their creativity and their education.
